About Us

More Than a Robot.
A Movement.

Visionary Sparks (FRC #11353) is a student-led robotics team united by curiosity, grit, and the belief that today's student engineers will shape tomorrow's world.

Founded with a mission to make cutting-edge STEM education accessible and exciting, we compete in our FIRST Robotics Competition — the world's premier high school robotics program — where students work alongside mentors and industry professionals to design, build, and program competition robots in just six weeks.

But we're more than our robot. We mentor younger students, host STEM outreach events, and foster a culture where every member — regardless of experience — can find their spark.

What is FIRST Robotics Competition?

FRC is the varsity sport of the mind. Each January, teams of 10–40+ students receive a game challenge and a kit of parts. In 6 weeks, they build a 120-lb robot capable of competing on a professional field. The season culminates in regional competitions and, for top teams, the FIRST Championship. Along the way, students gain real-world engineering skills, teamwork experience, and connections to the world's leading STEM companies.
