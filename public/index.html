About Us

More Than a Robot.
A Movement.

Visionary Sparks (FRC #11353) is a student-led robotics team united by curiosity, grit, and the belief that today's student engineers will shape tomorrow's world.

Founded to make advanced STEM learning accessible and exciting, we design, build, and program competition robots while learning how real engineering teams collaborate under pressure.

Our impact extends beyond the shop. We mentor younger students, host outreach events, and create space for every member to find their spark.

Why It Matters

  • VVision: we make decisions with long-term growth in mind.
  • EExcellence: we hold ourselves to real engineering and professional standards.
  • GGracious professionalism: we compete hard, respect people, and lift others up.
  • IImpact: success is measured by the people we inspire, not only by awards.
