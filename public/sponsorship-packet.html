Where Your Investment Goes

Impact

Your support funds more than a machine. It helps cover aluminum chassis materials, Java-based autonomous work, Limelight vision systems, competition travel and fees, and STEM outreach that builds the future technical workforce of our community.

All contributions are used for robot hardware, competition fees, and STEM outreach.
