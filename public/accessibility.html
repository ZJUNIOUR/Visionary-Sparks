Purpose and Applicable Standards

1. Purpose and Website Operator

Visionary Sparks FRC Team 11353 is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. This statement outlines our ongoing efforts to improve usability for all visitors.

This website is operated for Visionary Sparks FRC Team 11353 in connection with Halifax County Early College High School within Halifax County, North Carolina. School address: 100 College Drive, PO Drawer 809, Weldon, NC 27890. District oversight: Halifax County Schools, 9525 Hwy 301 South, Halifax, NC 27839. Technical maintainer and accessibility contact: Zabdiel Dewar (Coder), visionarysparksofficial@gmail.com.

2. Applicable Standards and Effective Communication

This website is designed with the goal of conforming, where reasonably practicable, to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

We aim to make current and commonly used website content, forms, and downloadable documents accessible in a manner consistent with WCAG 2.1 Level AA where reasonably practicable. If you need an accessible version of any current document or encounter a barrier, contact us and we will use reasonable efforts to provide effective communication promptly.

3

Accessibility Features

a. Structure and Navigation

  • 1Use of semantic HTML elements and structured headings
  • 2Consistent navigation across pages
  • 3Descriptive links and logical content organization

b. Keyboard Accessibility

  • 1Interactive elements are intended to be operable via keyboard
  • 2Visible focus indicators are provided where feasible

c. Readability

  • 1Efforts to maintain sufficient color contrast
  • 2Use of readable font sizes and clear language
  • 3Alternative text provided for images where practical

d. Reduced Motion

  • 1Support for reduced-motion user preferences to limit animations

Limitations and Ongoing Efforts

4. Limitations and Third-Party Content

Certain content may fall outside of our direct control or may still need additional remediation work, including downloadable documents such as PDFs or sponsor materials and embedded or linked third-party services such as CAPTCHA providers and social media platforms.

We are not responsible for the accessibility practices of third-party providers, but we encourage users to review their respective accessibility and support resources.

5. Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing process. Visionary Sparks will continue to evaluate and improve website accessibility as part of routine updates, content additions, document uploads, and design changes.
