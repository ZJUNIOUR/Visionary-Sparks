Retention, Student Content, and Third-Party Services

5. Public Records and Data Retention

Because this website may be operated in connection with a North Carolina public school unit or other public entity, certain submissions, emails, logs, approval records, and related website records may constitute public records and may be retained, produced, or disclosed as required by applicable law, court order, subpoena, audit, investigation, or approved records-retention schedules.

Contact-form submissions, related email correspondence, security logs, and delivery-failure backups are retained only for the period required to respond to the inquiry, protect the website, comply with applicable records schedules, or satisfy legal obligations. We may retain limited information after a deletion request to document compliance, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, or comply with records-retention requirements.

A proposed operating retention schedule is maintained with the school's website handoff records for administrative review before launch.

6. Student and Educational Content

This website may display student names, images, or team-related content. Student names, photos, videos, roster information, and other identifying team content should be published only after the responsible school, district, or organizational official confirms that disclosure is permitted under applicable media-release procedures, directory-information notices, opt-out elections, and any other required student privacy approvals.

Do not use the public contact form to send sensitive student records, including grades, discipline details, disability information, medical information, student identification numbers, or other confidential education records.

7. Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services, including hosting providers, email delivery services, fonts or content delivery networks, and hCaptcha, provided by Intuition Machines, Inc., to help prevent automated abuse of our forms.

These providers may process technical data as required to perform their services and operate under their own privacy policies. You can review hCaptcha's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

8-10

Rights, Children's Privacy, and Disclaimer

8. Your Rights and Requests

You may request that we delete your submitted information or provide information about data collected through your submission. Requests should be submitted using the contact information below.

We will make reasonable efforts to respond in a timely manner, but any deletion, restriction, or disclosure decision remains subject to applicable law, public-records obligations, legal holds, and school or district policy.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience, including students. If you are under 13, do not submit personal information through this website unless your parent, guardian, teacher, or an authorized school representative is involved. If we learn that we collected personal information directly from a child under 13 without any consent required by law, we will delete that information as soon as reasonably practicable unless retention is required by law.

10. Disclaimer

This website is provided for informational purposes. While reasonable efforts are made to protect user information, we do not guarantee absolute security or confidentiality beyond what applicable law allows.
