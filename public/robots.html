How We Build

From Strategy to Competition Readiness

Strategy

Game Analysis First

Before metal is cut or code is written, the team breaks down the challenge and defines the capabilities that matter most.

Design

Iterate Fast

Mechanical and programming leads compare options, prototype key actions, and narrow toward the most reliable design path.

Fabrication

Build for Reliability

Every subsystem is assembled with competition durability in mind so the drive team has a robot they can trust.

Software

Driver Confidence

Controls support, vision feedback, and autonomous logic are tuned to help operators make faster, better decisions.

Testing

Practice Under Pressure

We refine performance by running repeated drive, scoring, and repair cycles before competition.

Competition

Adapt in Real Time

Drive team strategy, technician support, and match review all feed back into how the robot performs on the field.
